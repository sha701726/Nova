Nova -- Rules & Code of Conduct
Nova is built on trust. Trust is not given freely -- it is earned through behaviour. Every coin in this system carries real monetary value of Rs 10,000. That weight comes with responsibility. These rules are not suggestions. They are the boundary between a functioning system and its collapse.
I. Foundation of Value
1
One Nova Coin is equal to Rs 10,000. This value is fixed and recognised within the Nova system. No participant may unilaterally redefine this value in any transaction, agreement, or claim.
2
Coins are earned through physical effort (10 km run) or purchased through the official payment gateway. There is no other legitimate source of coins.
3
Any coin obtained outside these two methods -- through exploits, manipulation, social engineering, or system abuse -- is considered void and grounds for permanent account removal.
II. Prohibited Conduct
4
Using Nova to receive payment for illegal goods, illegal services, or activity that violates Indian law is strictly forbidden. This includes but is not limited to: narcotics, weapons, extortion, and fraud.
5
Coercing, threatening, or pressuring another user into a transaction is a serious violation. All transfers must be voluntary and consented to by both parties.
6
Impersonating another user, using fake credentials, or creating accounts under false identity is prohibited. Each account must represent one real individual.
7
Attempting to manipulate GPS data, forge run sessions, exploit API endpoints, or interfere with the system in any technical manner constitutes abuse and will result in immediate account termination.
8
Sharing your password with any other person removes your protection under this system. You are solely responsible for all activity performed under your account.
III. Transactional Integrity
9
Every transaction recorded on Nova is permanent and cannot be reversed. Before confirming any payment, verify the receiver's identity and account number. Mistakes are your responsibility.
10
QR codes expire after 60 seconds. This is a security feature. Do not attempt to reuse, photograph, or redistribute a QR code to circumvent this expiry.
11
If you receive coins for a service or product, you are obligated to deliver on that commitment. Nova is not responsible for disputes between users, but repeated bad-faith actors will be removed.
IV. Earning Through Running
12
The running feature exists to reward real physical effort. It is an honour-based feature backed by technical anti-cheat measures including speed variance analysis, GPS accuracy checks, and time-window enforcement.
13
You must start your session before 5:00 AM IST and complete 10 km between 5:00 AM and 8:00 AM IST. Distance does not carry over between sessions. One coin per day maximum.
14
Driving, cycling, using a vehicle, or having another person carry your phone during a run session is cheating. The system will detect abnormal speed patterns. Accounts found cheating will be permanently banned.
V. Consequences
15
Violation of any rule above may result in: temporary suspension, permanent account removal, forfeiture of coin balance, or reporting to relevant authorities where applicable under Indian law.
16
Nova administrators reserve the right to investigate suspicious activity, freeze accounts under review, and make final decisions on rule violations. These decisions are not subject to appeal through the app.
VI. The Core Principle
If you would not do it in front of everyone using this system, do not do it here. Nova is a small community. Reputation travels fast. Act accordingly.
